Key Sections for Nurse CVs

  • Professional Licenses & Registrations: NELNEC Registration Number, Practicing License, Valid From/To dates
  • Clinical Specializations: ICU Nursing, Pediatrics, Maternity, Emergency, Surgical, Community Health
  • Technical Skills: Medical equipment proficiency, EHR systems, patient monitoring, medications
  • Work Experience: Patient load, unit types, nursing duties, achievements like "reduced patient complaints by 30%"
  • Education: Nursing degree, training programs, continuing education, specialized certifications
  • Soft Skills: Patient advocacy, teamwork, communication, crisis management, empathy

Common Mistakes Nurses Make on CVs

  • ❌ Underplaying patient outcomes and metrics
  • ❌ Not listing all relevant certifications and training
  • ❌ Poor formatting making licenses hard to find
  • ❌ Using vague descriptions ("handled patients") instead of specific actions
  • ❌ Forgetting to include continuing professional development
